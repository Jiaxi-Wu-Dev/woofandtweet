Audio projects are our passion

Lets create pleasing sounds for the ear

Woofer+Tweeter operates as a resource to those who wish to create music, record a podcast, find the perfect top liner, or discover their exact production needs, all in a way that makes sense for their budget and what they’re expressing.

Working primarily in analog and acoustic settings, I’ve focused on making the art of recording accessible and attainable for as many people as I possible can by providing basic information, thoughtful perspectives, and the practical magic that keeps small and large projects alive.

My goal is to serve independent artists who express themselves through recording. Respecting and nurturing their endeavor by providing my time inside and outside of the studio. I work best with those who have not been encouraged to or guided towards recording to capture their expressions or connect and promote other areas of their professional and creative lives.

I like to start by meeting with you and discussing what you’re interested in, how much time you have, and what you hope to experience, learn, or gain from your project. Then we’ll plan a second gathering to sketch out the building blocks of what achieving your vision will take. After that, we get to gettin’ while the gettin’s goooooooooooooooooooood.
